Data Deletion Instructions

According to Facebook Platform rules, we must provide a URL for users to request data deletion.

If you want to delete your activities for the LUX PRO Authentication Bridge, you can do so by following these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook Profile's Settings & Privacy. Click Settings.
  2. Look for "Apps and Websites" and you will see all of the apps and websites you linked with Facebook.
  3. Search and Click "LUX PRO Bridge" in the search bar.
  4. Scroll and click "Remove".
  5. Congratulations, you have successfully removed your app activities.
